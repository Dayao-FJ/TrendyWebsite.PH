The NVIDIA Journey

1993

Foundation

NVIDIA was founded by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem in California.

1999

GPU Pioneer

Launched the GeForce 256, the world's first GPU, changing the graphics industry.

2006

CUDA Revolution

Introduced CUDA, enabling GPUs to be used for general-purpose computing.

2016

AI Breakthrough

Launched the Pascal architecture, powering breakthroughs in deep learning and AI.

2022

Omniverse Expansion

Today

NVIDIA leads in AI, graphics, data centers, and autonomous systems, redefining the tech landscape.

About NVIDIA

NVIDIA Corporation is a U.S.-based multinational technology company known for inventing the GPU. Headquartered in Santa Clara, California, it has expanded its reach to AI, deep learning, autonomous vehicles, and data centers. With products like GeForce, RTX, and NVIDIA DGX systems, the company powers some of the most demanding computing applications worldwide.
