About Apple

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Renowned for its innovation in consumer electronics, software, and digital services, Apple’s product line includes the iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and Vision Pro. Its ecosystem integrates devices and services like iOS, macOS, and iCloud. With a focus on design, privacy, and sustainability, Apple continues to shape the digital age.
