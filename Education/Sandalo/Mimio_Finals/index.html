What is Mimio Teach?

MimioTeach is a portable device that transforms any standard dry-erase board into a fully interactive whiteboard. Utilizing patented infrared and ultrasound sensor technology, it allows teachers to interact with digital content projected onto the board.

Key features include:

  • Lightweight and easy to install: making it ideal for educators who move between classrooms.
  • Real-Time Rendering & Interactivity: Attaches magnetically to existing whiteboards and requires minimal setup.
  • Spatial Audio Integration : Provides interactive whiteboard functionality without the need for expensive installations.
  • Multi-Content Flexibility : Enables touch-based interaction with digital content, enhancing student engagement.

Mimio Interactive Whiteboard

How Does It Work?

Mimio Teach

MimioTeach uses infrared and ultrasound sensors to track the position of a stylus on the whiteboard, allowing for real-time interaction with projected content.

  1. Interactive Lessons: Teachers can write, draw, and manipulate objects on the board, making lessons more engaging.
  2. Collaborative Activities: Students can participate directly by using the stylus to solve problems or contribute ideas.
  3. Digital Annotation: Educators can annotate over digital content, such as websites or videos, to highlight key information.

The system integrates with MimioStudio software, enabling teachers to create, present, and save interactive lessons seamlessly.

When Was It Introduced?

MimioTeach was introduced in June 2010 during the ISTE conference in Denver, Colorado.

The development timeline included:

  • June 2010: Launch of MimioTeach, offering a portable solution for interactive teaching.
  • February 2016: Boxlight Corporation acquires Mimio, expanding its educational technology offerings.
  • 2020: Continued global implementation, with MimioTeach being used in classrooms across over 50 countries.

These milestones reflect MimioTeach's evolution and growing impact in enhancing interactive learning worldwide.
