About Mimio Teach

Mimio Teach, a flagship product under Boxlight Corporation, revolutionizes classrooms by turning traditional whiteboards into interactive learning spaces. Designed to be portable and teacher-friendly, Mimio Teach allows educators to engage students using digital tools without needing to replace existing whiteboards. Its wireless functionality, integration with lesson software, and emphasis on collaboration make it a standout in modern education. Today, Mimio products are trusted in classrooms around the world to deliver engaging, tech-enhanced instruction.

Driving Educational Innovation

As of 2025, Mimio remains committed to enhancing education through smart classroom technology. The MimioTeach system remains a staple, enabling teachers to use any standard whiteboard interactively. Complementing this are tools like MimioView for vivid lesson visuals, MimioPad for wireless classroom control, and MimioSpace for immersive group learning experiences. These solutions help educators engage students more effectively, whether in traditional classrooms or blended learning environments.
