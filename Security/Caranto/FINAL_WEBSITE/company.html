About NEC

NEC Corporation is a Japanese multinational IT and electronics company headquartered in Tokyo. It specializes in biometric security systems, artificial intelligence, network solutions, and social infrastructure technologies. NEC has earned global recognition for its leadership in facial recognition and ICT services, making significant contributions to safer, smarter societies worldwide.

Leadership & Vision

Kunihiko Iwadare

Founder

"Innovation in service to humanity has been NEC’s mission from the start."

Learn More

Kunihiko Iwadare, a visionary engineer, founded NEC in 1899 with the goal of advancing communication technology in Japan. Under his leadership, NEC grew from a small joint venture into a global innovator in IT and electronics. His legacy lives on through NEC’s commitment to technology that enhances lives and builds safer societies.
