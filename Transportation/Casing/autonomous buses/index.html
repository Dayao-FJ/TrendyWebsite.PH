Self-Driving Buses

Revolutionizing urban mobility in the Philippines

Zenmov's autonomous bus operating in New Clark City
September 2023 New Clark City, Philippines Public Transport

The country's first autonomous bus powered by Japanese technology is now operating in New Clark City, Clark Freeport Zone, and Clark International Airport, offering free rides to commuters during its initial phase.

Technology

This innovative project uses a transportation cloud system called Smart Mobility Operation Cloud to coordinate vehicle movements and ensure safe operation in urban environments.

Sustainability

The electric autonomous buses produce zero emissions during operation, contributing to cleaner air and reduced carbon footprint in Clark's growing urban center.

"We can definitely learn a lot from Japan's advanced and innovative transport system. By integrating their smart autonomous vehicle technology, we ensure a more sustainable and scalable transport solution here in Clark."

- Gisela Kalalo, BCDA Executive Vice President

Key Features

Connected Technology

Vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication enables real-time coordination with infrastructure and other vehicles.

Electric Power

Lithium-ion batteries provide clean energy with overnight charging capability.

Safety Systems

Multiple redundant systems including LIDAR, radar, and computer vision ensure passenger safety.
