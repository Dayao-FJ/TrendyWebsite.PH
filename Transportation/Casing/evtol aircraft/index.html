eVTOL Aircraft

The future of urban air mobility

Concept art of Archer's Midnight eVTOL aircraft
November 2023 Global Development Aviation

Electric Vertical Take-off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft are poised to revolutionize urban transportation with their zero-emission electric propulsion and vertical flight capabilities, potentially entering service as early as 2025.

How eVTOLs Work

1

Vertical Lift

Multiple electric motors power rotors for helicopter-like takeoff and landing

2

Transition

Rotors tilt or adjust for forward flight after takeoff

3

Cruise

Aircraft flies like a plane using wing lift for efficiency
